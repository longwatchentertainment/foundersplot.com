A game that respects
how business actually works.

Most farming games give you an idle loop and call it a business. Founder's Plot gives you cash flow problems, reputation risk, and the specific satisfaction of watching a wholesale contract pay off after three weeks of careful planning.

You can go bankrupt. You can recover. The post-mortem tells you exactly where the money went and what to do differently. That's a feature, not a bug.

Built by a solo developer, on evenings, over two years. Designed to be the game that didn't exist yet.

The invention system

Describe a product. We'll tell you if it works.

Type a product in plain language. The AI evaluates feasibility, balances the economics against your equipment tier, and either approves it — or explains honestly why it doesn't work. Approved products are yours. License them to other players and earn royalties every time someone makes a batch.
